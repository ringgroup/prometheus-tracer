Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

Prometheus Tracer ("the Service") is operated by Ring Labs ("we", "us"). This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. The Service is a personal body‑composition tracker; you control the data you put into it.

Information we collect

  • Account info (via GitHub). When you sign in with GitHub we receive your GitHub username, display name, and avatar URL using the read:user scope only. We do not receive your password or your private repositories.
  • Data you enter. The measurements you log (weight, muscle mass, body‑fat %, dates) and the injections/protocol entries you log (compound, dose, date). This is the data the Service exists to track.
  • API tokens. If you connect an AI client, we store the access token(s) you generate so requests can be authenticated. You can revoke them at any time.
  • A session cookie. One signed, HttpOnly cookie to keep you signed in. No third‑party advertising or analytics cookies.

How we use your information

  • To authenticate you and show you your own data.
  • To serve your data to an AI client only when you explicitly connect one with your token (read‑only).
  • To operate, secure, and maintain the Service.

We do not sell your data, share it for advertising, or use it to train models.

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in private object storage on Vercel, which also hosts the Service. Authentication is provided by GitHub. These providers act as our sub‑processors and process data on our behalf. Data is segregated per user and only returned to a request that proves it is yours (a valid session or your API token).

Your choices and rights

  • Edit or delete entries at any time from the dashboard.
  • Revoke API tokens from the “Connect AI” panel.
  • Sign out to end your session.
  • Delete your account/data: request full deletion via the contact below and we will remove your stored tracker data and tokens.

Retention

We keep your data until you delete it or request account deletion. Revoked tokens are deleted immediately.

Health disclaimer

Prometheus Tracer is a self‑tracking tool, not a medical device or a source of medical advice. Nothing in the Service is a recommendation to use any substance. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional. See our Terms.

Children

The Service is not directed to anyone under 18 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
